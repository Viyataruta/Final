Boiler Social

Help Page

This platform is designed to help Purdue students find friends.
This is supposed to be a play on words related to the beloved Purdue mascot, the BoilerMaker Special. Instead of a train to connect students, it's a social media website designed to connect students to their peers.
The purpose of this is to make it safer for students, ensuring only Purdue students can register. This will prevent bots. Students will also have more ethical behavior online if they know their activity is tied to their school email.
